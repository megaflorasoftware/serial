## Features

- Serial now supports generic RSS feeds, specifically geared towards including articles and blog posts
- Serial now supports customizing the open location of each feed, letting you choose whether to open it in Serial or at the origin link
  - This can be especially useful for articles and blog posts, where RSS feeds can often contain incomplete content

## Improvements

- Data is persisted locally between sessions, speeding startup times

## Tweaks & Improvements (2025-08-31)

- The "Inbox" time window is now "One Month"
- Articles now include the `description` field for items (as a fallback for page content) if the RSS feed has it
- Better support for Atom feeds
- Article and Video zoom values are now separate

## Features

### Background Refresh

- Background refresh is now available for self-hosted instances. This will automatically fetch new content for your feeds as it becomes available, instead of requiring you to wait on startup to fetch new content.
- This can be enabled with `BACKGROUND_REFRESH_ENABLED=true` in your environment variables.

### Feed activity

- Added the ability to disable/enable feeds. This allows you to keep feeds in your feed list, but prevent new content from being fetched (until turned back on)

## Improvements

- Desktop actions now only appear on hover, giving more space to page content
  - You can always show actions by setting "Inline Shortcuts" to "Show" in the Appearance settings, or by holding <kbd>Alt</kbd> to show all keyboard shortcuts.

## Fixes

- <kbd>Alt</kbd> key now only shows shortcuts if it is the only key being held
- Item selection UI no longer shows on mobile, as it is not needed
- Fixed an issue where articles and videos were a bit jumpy on initial load

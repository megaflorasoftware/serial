## Features

### Saved progress across videos and articles

- Progress is now saved for videos and articles
- For videos, Serial will now seek to your last position when playing for the first time
- For articles, Serial will jump to the section you left off at last
- Content with progress information will show a progress bar on the root page

## Improved view navigation

- Added <kbd>[</kbd> and <kbd>]</kbd> keyboard shortcuts to cycle between views, in addition to the existing number shortcuts (<kbd>0</kbd> through <kbd>9</kbd>)

## Other changes

- Reverted a change where opened items would be marked as read automatically

#### Account Management

- Users can now update their email
- Users can now update their name
- Users can now update their password from the app
- Users can now delete their accounts

#### Shortcuts

- On small desktop screens, the right sidebar can now be toggled with `Shift` + `\` or the `|` key (you can toggle the left with just `\`)
- You can now refresh feeds with `r`

#### Fixes

- Fixes an issue where viewing a video from outside a user's feeds would crash the app
